Welcome to Bulls and Cows Game!

In this game, you'll try to guess a 4-digit number. Here’s what each column means:

  • Bulls → The number of digits that are correct and in the correct position.
  • Cows → The number of digits that are correct but in the wrong position.
  • Guess → Your guessed 4-digit number.
  • Attempt → The number of times you've guessed.

Once you're ready, click "OK" to start the game!
